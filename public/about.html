About Me

What can you say about yourself, Guilherme?

A: I think the best way to answer that would be to say that I'm a multi-faceted person. I love coding, gaming, parties, art.

I've always been that quiet kid who got fascinated by all sorts of things, and as such, wanted to understand and learn it all. Probably that's the reason I have so many different hobbies, trying to experience all of it as much as possible, you only get one shot.

When did you start working with tech?

A: It was my mother's idea. She always said I was brilliant, and as such, I should aspire to great things, which led me to try my hand at everything I liked, with various degrees of sucess. Tech was a big hit, and she said that since I was so good with machines, maybe I should look into IT as a career.

My mom always said that I first learned to use computers, and only after that I decided it was time to learn how to walk, so here we are. I got into a few different courses, trying to see what I liked most in IT, and I ended up falling for Games, AI's and Web Design. That's how I came to be as a professional.

And who are you when VS Code isn't around?

A: I'm an art lover. Singing, dancing, playing, acting, photographing, filming. Art was always my biggest passion since I was a lad, you could say I'm a theater kid at heart (chuckles). But I'm also fascinated by all sorts of games, both digital and tabletop ones. D&D, Dungeons and Dragons, is one of my favorites, as well as the ones from my childhood, like Yu-Gi-Oh and Minecraft. In my spare time, I tend to look for monologues to practise, or new theater plays or gameplays to watch. And with my music school, we try and make some small scale concerts for charity, so I've been known to be a philantropist every now and then.

I've been trying my hand at a few theater companies lately, but so far, haven't had much luck, unfortunately haha.
